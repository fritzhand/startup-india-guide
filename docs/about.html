About this playbook

A navigable edition of the Government of India's Playbook of Government Schemes and Initiatives for Startups (June 2026).

Objective

This Playbook consolidates Central Government schemes and incentives relevant to India's startup ecosystem into a single, usable reference document. It is designed to help entrepreneurs find the right scheme for their stage and sector.

Who it is for

  • Entrepreneurs & Founders
  • Students & Aspiring Entrepreneurs
  • Investors & Incubators
  • Policymakers & Intermediaries

How to use it

  1. Start with the Decision Tree. Answer 5 quick questions to find schemes suited to you.
  2. Check the "What Do You Need?" Index for a need-based view (grant, equity, loan, etc.).
  3. Use the Lifecycle Map to find schemes matching your stage of growth.
  4. Explore the Summary Table for a structured overview of all schemes with key details.
  5. Deep dive into Scheme One-Pagers for full scheme details including how to apply.

Startup focus legend

Startup-Specific — startups are the primary or sole eligible beneficiary explicitly named in scheme eligibility; Startup-Relevant — broader eligibility (MSMEs, companies, institutions) but startups regularly benefit and apply.

How it is organised

The front matter comprises a disclaimer (page 2), an 'About this Playbook' overview with headline stats, support categories, startup lifecycle coverage and industry/sector coverage (page 5), the Objective / Who It Is For / How to Use This Playbook / Focus Legend page (page 6), and a two-page List of Abbreviations of Schemes and Programmes covering roughly 50 scheme acronyms from SISFS through ESDP (pages 7-8). The body then begins at page 9 with SECTION 1 'Quick Navigation' ('Your fastest route to the right scheme / program'), which per the how-to-use guidance contains the Decision Tree, the 'What Do You Need?' need-based index, the Lifecycle Map and the Summary Table, followed later by detailed Scheme One-Pagers with full scheme details including how to apply.

The six types of support

GRANT

Fund given with no repayment needed, no equity given up.

EQUITY

Investment in exchange for a stake in your startup.

LOAN / CREDIT

Repayable loan or credit guarantee to help access debt.

INCUBATION

Access to lab, workspace, mentoring and ecosystem support.

MARKET ACCESS

Pathways to sell, export, or access procurement markets.

MIXED

Combination of the above as well any other types of support provided to startups.
