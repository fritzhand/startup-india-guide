SISFS · Part AMixed

Startup India Seed Fund Scheme

Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT)

Get up to ₹20 lakh in grants and ₹50 lakh in investment to validate your idea, build a prototype, and enter the market.

Prototype / PoCSeed / Early-StageMarket Access & IP Up to ₹50 lakh
FFS · Part AEquity

Fund of Funds for Startups

Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT)

A ₹10,000 crore fund that backs SEBI-registered venture funds (AIFs), which in turn invest equity in DPIIT-recognised startups.

Growth / Scaling Corpus of ₹10,000 crore
CGSS · Part ALoan / Credit

Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups

Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT)

Government guarantees up to ₹20 crore on loans to DPIIT-recognised startups, so lenders can fund you without collateral.

Seed / Early-StageGrowth / Scaling Guarantee up to ₹20 crore
iDEX · Part AGrant

Innovations for Defence Excellence

Ministry of Defence / Defence Innovation Organisation (DIO)

Defence innovation grants of ₹1.5–10 crore plus incubator support for startups and MSMEs solving military challenges.

Prototype / PoCMarket Access & IP Grants of ₹1.5 crore to ₹10 crore
BIG · Part AGrant

Biotechnology Ignition Grant Scheme

Department of Biotechnology / Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC)

Grant-in-aid of up to ₹50 lakh over 18 months for biotech startups and entrepreneurs to turn scientific ideas into products.

IdeationPrototype / PoC Grant up to ₹50 lakh
SAMRIDH · Part AEquity

Startup Accelerators of MeitY for Product Innovation, Development & Growth

Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY)

Up to ₹40 lakh investment plus equal matching private funding and acceleration services to scale your IT product startup.

Growth / Scaling Up to ₹40 lakh + matching ₹40 lakh
